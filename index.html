The House of PHZE

PHZE 3 — Producer turned artist, master of dark, moody R&B with a sharp edge.

Joey, known professionally as PHZE 3, emerged from behind the boards as a skilled producer and beatmaker into a compelling solo artist voice. Rooted deeply in the underground R&B scene, PHZE 3 crafts music that explores raw emotions, toxic love, and late-night confessions with a sonic palette that’s both hypnotic and haunting.

His sound is a signature blend of smoky, atmospheric beats layered with seductive melodies and complex lyrical flows that tap into the vulnerable yet unapologetic heart of modern urban life. Influenced by the moodiness of classic R&B and the modern grit of artists like Drake, PHZE 3’s music dives into the darkness of passion, heartbreak, and the search for meaning in fleeting connections.

As the visionary behind the “Heartless Club” movement, PHZE 3 builds a sonic universe where love is complicated, loyalty is scarce, and emotions run deep — all with a sleek, polished production style honed through years of studio experience. His evolution from producer to frontman reflects a dedication to authenticity, craftsmanship, and storytelling that resonates with anyone who’s ever loved, lost, or been left in the fire.

Whether he’s delivering raw bars or crafting immersive soundscapes, PHZE 3 stands out as a rising force in contemporary R&B — a voice for the night owls, the broken-hearted, and the unapologetically real.
